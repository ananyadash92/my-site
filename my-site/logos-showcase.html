Recommendation

Logo V1 (Mark + Wordmark) is the stronger choice for the website header. The "bridge" mark gives you a recognisable favicon and social icon. The mark concept — two brackets bridged by an amber line — directly encodes your value proposition. V2 is excellent for print collateral and presentation title slides where the full typographic treatment has room to breathe.
