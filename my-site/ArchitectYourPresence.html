Why Image Coaching?

Your image speaks before you do. Make sure it tells the right story.

Research shows 55% of first impressions are based on appearance, 38% on tone of voice, and only 7% on words. Yet most professionals invest everything in skills and nothing in how they present those skills to the world.

As an ICF-trained coach with a deep instinct for style, I don't just pick outfits — I help you understand the psychology behind your personal presentation. We work on the complete picture: how you look, how you carry yourself, and how you communicate.

This isn't superficial — it's strategic. When your outer presence matches your inner capability, you become congruent. And congruence is what makes people trust, follow, and remember you.

FAQ

Questions You Might Have

Is image coaching just about clothes?

Not at all. Clothes are one element. We work on the complete ABC — Appearance, Behaviour, and Communication. It's a holistic approach to your personal brand backed by ICF coaching methodology.

I'm a man — is this for me too?

Absolutely. Image coaching is for anyone who wants their external presence to match their internal capability. Men's grooming, suiting, business casual, and executive presence are core areas I cover.

How is this different from hiring a stylist?

A stylist picks outfits. I coach you to understand why certain choices work, build lasting habits, and align your image with your goals. The change is permanent, not dependent on me being there.

What does ICF coaching have to do with image?

Image blocks are often identity blocks. ICF coaching methodology helps us explore the deeper beliefs holding you back — not just "what to wear" but "who do you want to become?" That's where lasting transformation lives.

Can we work together online?

Yes! Most of my coaching sessions happen over video call. For wardrobe audits, you'll share photos or do a live walkthrough on camera. Corporate workshops can be in-person or virtual.
